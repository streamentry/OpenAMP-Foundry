# Safety Policy

## Position

OpenAMP Foundry exists to accelerate responsible antimicrobial discovery while reducing misuse risk.

The repo is designed for dry-lab candidate triage. It does not provide wet-lab protocols, pathogen-handling instructions, or harmful optimization objectives.

## Safety defaults

- Safety scores are penalizing, not optimizing harmful traits.
- Candidate certificates must list failure modes.
- Unscreened candidate lists are not published by default.
- Generator weights are not shipped in this repo.
- Dangerous organism protocols are out of scope.
- Contributors must not add content that materially increases biological misuse capability.

## Disallowed contributions

Pull requests may be rejected if they add:

- toxicity-maximizing objectives;
- instructions for culturing or modifying pathogens;
- high-risk assay protocols;
- unscreened candidate dumps;
- evasion of safety filters;
- claims of efficacy without assay evidence;
- clinical or medical advice.

## Review rule

When in doubt, prefer slower release over unrestricted release.
