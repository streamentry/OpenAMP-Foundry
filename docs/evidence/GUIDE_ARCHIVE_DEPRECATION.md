# Archive and Deprecation Guide

How to archive and deprecate documents or features.

## Deprecation Process
1. Add a deprecation notice to the document or feature.
2. Update references to point to the replacement.
3. Announce the deprecation in the decision log.
4. After one review cycle, archive the document.

## Archive Process
1. Move the document to `docs/archive/` with a date prefix.
2. Update PROJECT_INDEX.md to mark it as archived.
3. Remove or redirect cross-references.
4. Add the archive entry to the archive index.

## What NOT to Archive
- Decision logs
- Evidence documents with historical benchmark results
- Documents referenced by external publications
