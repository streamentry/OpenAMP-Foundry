# Non-Goals Page

What the project explicitly does NOT aim to do.

## Non-Goals
1. **Replace wet-lab validation** — computational scores are predictions, not proof.
2. **Discover drugs** — the pipeline nominates candidates, not drugs.
3. **Guarantee safety** — safety scores are heuristics, not guarantees.
4. **Replace domain experts** — expert review is required for all releases.
5. **Optimize for toxicity** — that would violate the safety charter.
6. **Operate autonomously** — human review is required for all decisions.

## Why Document Non-Goals
- Prevents mission creep.
- Sets clear expectations for users and contributors.
- Makes it easier to say no to out-of-scope work.
- Aligns the project with its safety and honesty constraints.
