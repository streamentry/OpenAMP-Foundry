# Metric Guide: Maintenance Health

**Question:** Are the docs staying current, or are they accumulating stale content?

**What to track:** Age of last significant update per document.

**Target:** No document older than 6 months without review.

**How to measure:** Check `git log --oneline docs/` for each document.

**What good looks like:** Active documents are updated within the last 3 months. Stale documents are flagged for review or retirement.
