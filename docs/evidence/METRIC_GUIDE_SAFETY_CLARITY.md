# Metric Guide: Safety Clarity

**Question:** Can a reviewer find the safety policy and understand what it permits?

**What to track:** Time to find and understand the safety policy.

**Target:** < 5 minutes.

**What to measure:** A new reader can find SAFETY.md, identify allowed and forbidden uses, and state the dry-lab boundary within 5 minutes.

**What good looks like:** Reviewer can quote the relevant policy section without assistance.
