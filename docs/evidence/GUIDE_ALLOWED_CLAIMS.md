# Allowed Public Claim Examples

Examples of allowed and forbidden public claims.

## Allowed
- "Computationally nominated 24 antimicrobial peptide candidates."
- "Pipeline achieves AUROC 0.78 on benchmark — limitations apply."
- "All candidates are dry-lab predictions requiring wet-lab validation."
- "Full reproducible evidence trail available for scientific review."

## Forbidden
- "Discovered a new antibiotic." (Requires wet-lab validation)
- "Pipeline-validated antimicrobial." (Computation does not validate)
- "Safe, effective peptide therapy." (No clinical evidence)
- "AI drug discovery breakthrough." (Overclaiming)

## Rule
When in doubt, use the weakest accurate claim. Add a caveat.
