# Evidence Boundary Explainer

What counts as evidence in the OpenAMP Foundry pipeline, and what does not.

## Computational Evidence (Levels 0-4)
- Valid sequence syntax
- Reproducible features
- Baseline heuristic scores
- Multi-model consensus
- Leakage-resistant benchmark results

## Biological Evidence (Levels 5-6)
- Lab assay results
- Independent replication

## The Boundary
Computational evidence is sufficient for triage and nomination.
Biological evidence is required for any claim of activity, safety, or efficacy.
Crossing the boundary without wet-lab data is the most common and
most serious form of overclaiming in AI-driven discovery.
