# Reviewer Questionnaire Guide

Questions a reviewer should be able to answer after reviewing a PR.

## For the Change
- Does the change do what it claims?
- Are there any side effects or regressions?
- Is the change backward-compatible?

## For Safety
- Does the change introduce overclaiming language?
- Does the change affect safety policy?
- Does the change require a safety review?

## For Quality
- Are there tests for the change?
- Do the tests pass?
- Is the documentation updated?
