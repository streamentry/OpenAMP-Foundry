# Issue Closing Policy

When to close issues and how.

## Valid Closing Reasons

- The change has been implemented and merged.
- The issue is a duplicate of another issue.
- The issue is out of scope for the project.
- The issue cannot be reproduced.
- The issue has been deferred and is tracked in a milestone.

## Required Information

- The closing comment should explain why the issue is being closed.
- If the issue was resolved by a PR, link the PR.
- If the issue was deferred, link the tracking milestone or epic.

## Not Valid Reasons

- "We don't have time for this" — assign to a milestone instead.
- "This is too hard" — break into smaller issues.
