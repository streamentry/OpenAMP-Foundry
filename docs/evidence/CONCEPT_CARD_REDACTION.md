# Redaction — Concept Card

Redaction removes sensitive information from documents before public sharing.

## What May Need Redaction

- Full peptide sequences for dual-use candidates
- Specific assay protocols from partner CROs
- Personally identifiable information
- Unpublished candidate lists before review

## Rules

- Redacted content should be replaced with `[REDACTED]` or a summary.
- The fact that redaction occurred should be noted.
- Redaction must not hide negative results or safety issues.
- The original unredacted version must be preserved in a restricted location.
