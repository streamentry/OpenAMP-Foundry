# Retention Policy Explanation

How long documents and artifacts are retained.

## Retention Periods
| Type | Retention | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Evidence certificates | Permanent | Historical record |
| Decision logs | Permanent | Governance record |
| Benchmark results | Permanent | Scientific record |
| Policy docs | Permanent | Until superseded |
| Run manifests | 2 years | Reproducibility |
| Temporary outputs | 90 days | Outputs/ directory |
| Discussion comments | 1 year | After issue closes |

## Disposal
After the retention period, documents are archived. Archived documents
are never deleted without a decision log entry explaining why.
