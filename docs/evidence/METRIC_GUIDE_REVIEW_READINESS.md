# Metric Guide: Review Readiness

**Question:** Is the project ready for external review?

**What to track:** Whether all review artifacts exist and are up to date.

**Artifacts to check:**
- docs/review/EXPERT_REVIEW_PACK.md
- docs/review/RELEASE_DRY_RUN_GUIDE.md
- docs/review/PUBLICATION_PACK.md
- docs/evidence/LIMITATIONS_OVERVIEW.md
- .github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/expert_review.yml

**Target:** All 5 artifacts exist and pass claim check.

**What good looks like:** A reviewer can be pointed to the review pack and have everything they need.
