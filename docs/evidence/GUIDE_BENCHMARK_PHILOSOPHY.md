# Benchmark Philosophy Page

The project's approach to benchmarking.

## Principles
1. Benchmarks measure discrimination, not biological truth.
2. Every benchmark has caveats that must be documented.
3. No single benchmark determines pipeline quality — use the full suite.
4. Benchmarks must be reproducible (deterministic, versioned inputs).
5. Benchmarks must be adversarial — actively look for ways the pipeline is wrong.

## What Benchmarks Do NOT Measure
- Real-world antimicrobial activity
- Safety in humans
- Clinical utility
- Synthesis success rate
- Actual hit rate in a screening campaign
