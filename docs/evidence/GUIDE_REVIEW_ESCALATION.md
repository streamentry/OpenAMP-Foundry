# Review Escalation Guide

When a review is stuck or contested.

## Escalation Levels
| Level | Who | When |
|-------|-----|------|
| 1 | PR author and reviewer | Initial disagreement |
| 2 | Additional reviewer | 2 reviewers disagree |
| 3 | Maintainer | Review blocked for > 48h |
| 4 | Project lead | Policy or safety disagreement |

## Process
1. Document the disagreement in PR comments.
2. Attempt resolution at the current level.
3. Escalate only after good-faith attempts fail.
4. Higher-level decisions are final.
