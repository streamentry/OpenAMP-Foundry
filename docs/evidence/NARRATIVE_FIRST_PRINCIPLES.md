# First-Principles Project Narrative

OpenAMP Foundry exists to answer one question: Can an open, reproducible,
safety-constrained computational pipeline nominate antimicrobial peptide
candidates that are worth testing in a lab?

## Why This Matters
Antimicrobial resistance is a global threat. Current discovery methods are slow
and expensive. Computation can help triage candidates, but only if the pipeline
is honest about its limitations.

## Our Approach
1. Transparent heuristics, not black-box models.
2. Benchmark honesty over impressive metrics.
3. Safety constraints built into the scoring.
4. Reproducibility as a first-class requirement.
5. Human review as a mandatory gate.

## Our Non-Goal
We do not claim that computation replaces biology. The lab is the judge.
