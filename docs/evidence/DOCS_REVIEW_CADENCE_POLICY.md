# Documentation Review Cadence Policy

Documents are reviewed on a regular cadence to ensure freshness.

## Cadence

| Document Type | Review Cadence | Trigger |
|---------------|:--------------:|---------|
| Policy docs | Every 6 months | Calendar reminder |
| Getting-started guides | Every 3 months | Or after a CLI change |
| Evidence docs | Every 6 months | Or after a benchmark change |
| Concept cards | Annually | Or after a related policy change |
| Runbooks | Every 3 months | Or after a workflow change |

## Process

1. Reviewer checks accuracy, completeness, and link health.
2. Outdated docs are updated or flagged for retirement.
3. Review results are recorded in the decision log.
