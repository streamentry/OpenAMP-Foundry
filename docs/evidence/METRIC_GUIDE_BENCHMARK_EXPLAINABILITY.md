# Metric Guide: Benchmark Explainability

**Question:** Can a reader understand what each benchmark measures and its limitations?

**What to track:** Whether each benchmark in BENCHMARKING.md has a caveat section.

**Target:** Every benchmark has at least one documented caveat.

**What to measure:** Ratio of benchmarks with caveats to total benchmarks.

**What good looks like:** A reader can find any benchmark and immediately see what it does NOT prove.
