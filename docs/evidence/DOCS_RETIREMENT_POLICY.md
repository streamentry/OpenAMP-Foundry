# Documentation Retirement Policy

Documents that are no longer accurate or useful should be retired, not
left to accumulate.

## When to Retire

- The document describes a process that no longer exists.
- The document has been superseded by a newer version.
- The document is inaccurate and cannot be fixed.
- The document has no identifiable audience.

## Process

1. Add a deprecation notice at the top of the document.
2. Update cross-references to point to the replacement.
3. After one review cycle, move the document to an archive directory.
4. After two review cycles, delete the document.

## What Not to Retire

- Evidence documents with historical benchmark results.
- Decision logs.
- Documents that are referenced by external publications.
