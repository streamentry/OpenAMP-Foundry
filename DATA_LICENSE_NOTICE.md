# Data License Notice

This repository ships only toy/demo data.

External AMP databases and benchmarks may have their own licenses, terms of access, citation requirements, or redistribution limits. Do not assume you can redistribute third-party biological datasets.

Recommended policy:

1. Provide fetch scripts where allowed.
2. Store dataset metadata and source URLs.
3. Store licenses and access dates.
4. Avoid committing raw third-party data unless redistribution is clearly permitted.
5. Add a dataset card for every source.

Dataset cards should include:

- source name;
- source URL;
- access date;
- license or terms;
- redistribution status;
- citation requirements;
- label definitions;
- known biases;
- preprocessing steps.
